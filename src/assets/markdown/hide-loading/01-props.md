Just like `showLoading`, this will hide the loading animation for a component. Best to use this when your promise resolves.

<br>

---

<br>

## Props

<br>

**id** `string`<br>
The ID of the component you wish to stop loading

---

<br>

## Returns

<br>

`void`<br>
Does not return anything.
